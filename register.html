Financial Assistance

PMC has a few funds, each with a unique purpose.

Alison Stewart-Patterson fund

The ASP fund provides bursaries for individuals or families who need financial assistance with camp fees.

Gladys E. Montgomery fund

The GEM fund provides support for campers with special needs in the form of a stipend for an assistant or caregiver.

Moncrieff Memorial fund

The Moncrieff Memorial fund provides resources to purchase musical instruments for the camp, with a particular focus on rhythm instruments for younger campers. For inquiries, please contact the treasurer at pmctreasurer@gmail.com.

Tom Gemmell fund

The Tom Gemmell fund provide bursaries to cover the cost of attending PMC for one member of the clergy or certified student, and one music director.
