Visitors

If you want to visit PMC, the fees are:

 Daily Rate*Meals
Adult (13+)$120.00$40.00/meal
Youth (5-12)$100.00$20.00meal

Children 4 and under are free. There is no HST to be added.

*The Daily Rate includes overnight accommodation and 3 meals.

If you are thinking about staying overnight, please talk to the Registrar (Cathy Whiteside) before you arrive. The options for your accommodation are very limited. The Registrar will have the most up to date information for you.
