PMC Covenant (Code of Conduct)

By participating in PMC, you agree to its Covenant. 

  1. Treat others as you would like to be treated, both online and in person.
  2. Help create a safe, inclusive and fun environment for all.
  3. Treat Tamarack and PMC staff and volunteers with respect and appreciation.
  4. Respect the privacy and security of other campers’ cabins and other campers in your cabin.
  5. Be mindful of other campers’ need for quiet and sleep.
  6. Demonstrate care and respect for instruments, equipment, property and the environment.
  7. Report anything that concerns you to a PMC Board member or trusted adult.
